The [secretary problem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secretary_problem), do you hire the current candidate or do you wait for the next candidate that might be 2-3x better. 

Big tech companies will evaluate their products the same way. They need the investment to generate a big return for the company for them to pursue the project.
